About Me

Julieta Sanzol digital Artist

My name is Julieta Sanzol, I am from Argentina, specifically Buenos Aires. I am 21 years old and have been drawing since I was approximately 10 years old. I started drawing traditionally and at the end of 2023 I discovered the world of digital illustration. I automatically felt an incredible connection and confort, as if I had drawn this way my entire life. As soon as I decided that i would continue down this path, I began to educated myself and study a lot. I was exploring styles that suited my tastes and today I continue to learn a lot, but I am getting closer to my goals. I am studying animation and digital art at UNLAM. Along the path of illustration I also discovered that I wanted to give more life to my drawings, tell a story, convey what I am really feeling with each strokes and be able to carry out projects both personal and forgot other peoples who are locking for the same things.

For this reason, I feel fulfilled drawing and I enjoy every detail that this process entails where I only find myself, the pencil and my love for this art.
